Checkpoint Projects

Mobile-first project tracker. No accounts. No cloud. Just checkboxes.

checkpointprojects.com Live Proprietary
Personal Projects Task Management Mobile-Friendly Works Offline

Checkpoint Projects is a lightweight, offline-first project manager that gets out of your way. Create projects with nested task checklists and watch animated progress bars fill up as you complete work. Everything is stored locally on your device — no account required, no data sent to servers, no analytics. Sync between devices using a simple 6-character code when you need it, on your terms.

Built as a direct alternative to bloated subscription tools: you buy it once and own it forever. Export your data at any time to YAML, JSON, or CSV — no lock-in.

ConsistencyHub

Keep your stories consistent. Track characters, events, and factions — AI optional.

consistencyhub.io Live Proprietary
Fiction Writers TTRPG Game Masters Screenwriters Zero-Knowledge

ConsistencyHub is a zero-knowledge document consistency platform for writers and world-builders. It stores everything directly in your OneDrive — no backend, no server-side storage, no one but you can read your work. Smart heuristics automatically catch contradictions in your documents (a character described as dead appearing alive in a later chapter, faction alliances flipped, location details mismatched) before your readers do.

Write in a full DOCX editor with live consistency highlighting, manage your world bible in an Excel-like spreadsheet, visualise entity relationships in an interactive knowledge graph, and generate characters, factions, locations, and events with ease. AI assistance is entirely optional — bring your own API key (ChatGPT, Claude, or others) only if you want it.

A Microsoft account is required for OneDrive storage (free to create).
